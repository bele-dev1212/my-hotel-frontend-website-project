Welcome to Grand Hotel

Where Tradition Meets Modern Luxury

Established in 1925, Grand Hotel has been a beacon of hospitality for nearly a century. Our historic property combines old-world charm with contemporary comforts to create an unforgettable experience for every guest.

Nestled in the heart of the city, we offer easy access to cultural landmarks while providing a tranquil retreat from the bustling streets.
