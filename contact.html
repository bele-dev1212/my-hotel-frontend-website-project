Our Location

Find us in the heart of the city

By Subway

Take the 1, 2, 3, 7, N, Q, R, or W trains to Times Square-42nd Street station. We're a 5-minute walk from the station.

By Bus

Multiple bus lines stop near the hotel including M5, M7, M20, M42, and M104. Check current schedules for details.

By Car

Valet parking is available at the hotel entrance. Nearby parking garages are also available on 44th and 45th streets.

From Airports

30-45 minutes from JFK, LGA, and EWR airports. Private transfers can be arranged through our concierge.

Quick answers to common questions
